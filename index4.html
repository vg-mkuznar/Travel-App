★ 4.7
Easy · Crestline · 20 min west
Heart Rock Trail
1.8-mile loop along Seeley Creek through black oaks and Jeffrey pines. Ends at a heart-shaped hole in rock next to a 20-ft waterfall. Early May is prime — waterfall is likely still flowing. Blue hearts painted on rocks keep you on route. Perfect pug trail.
1.8 mi loop Easy--Moderate ~45 min Free · Year-round
Open in Maps ›
🐾 Pug Notes
Both trails require a leash. Heart Rock is the better pug trail — shaded creek path, gentle grade, easy terrain. Pinnacles has more elevation gain and scrambling near the top — watch your pug on the rocks. Bring water for both of you.
